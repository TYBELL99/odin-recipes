Slow Cooker Chicken Tacos

Description

These slow cooker chicken tacos are tender, juicy, and full of flavor - the perfect set -it-and-forget-it meal for busy days. Just add your favorite toppings and enjoy a no-fuss dinner everyone will one.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 teaspoons taco seasoning mix
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl
  3. Place chicken in a slow cooker
  4. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken
  5. Cook on low for 6 to 8 hours
  6. Shred chicken with two forks
